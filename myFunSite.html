Our Story

02.01.2017

We believe that music should be communal yet individual. Getting together at music festivals and concerts, we discussed how we learn about new artists and share music. We compiled a list of pros and cons of the main music apps we used and created JJamz hoping for users to explore and share their unique tastes!

How to Use

It's Super Easy!

  • Step 1: go to www.jjamz.com or download the jjamz app available on both iTunes and Android markets

  • Step 2: Login through your Facebook or start a new account through your email

  • Step 3: Follow your friends, artists, or other users & get started!
